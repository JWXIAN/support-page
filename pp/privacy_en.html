Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Introduction

PP (hereinafter referred to as "we" or "the App") understands the importance of your personal information. We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

This Privacy Policy will help you understand how we collect, use, and store your personal information, as well as the rights you hold. Please read and understand this Privacy Policy carefully before using the App.

1. Information We Collect

This App does not collect or upload any user personal information. Specifically:

  • No Identity Information: We do not collect your name, ID number, passport number, home address, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • No Location Data: The App does not request or collect your geographic location information.
  • No Device Information: The App does not collect your device identifier, IP address, operating system version, or any other device information.
  • No Usage Data: The App does not use any third-party analytics tools and does not track your usage behavior.

2. Data Storage

All data generated within the App (including but not limited to visa assessment results, checklists, history records, and settings preferences) is stored exclusively in the following locations:

  • On-Device Storage: Assessment data, settings, etc. are stored locally on your iPhone.
  • Your Personal iCloud Account: If you subscribe to Pro and enable iCloud Sync, relevant data will be synced to your personal iCloud account via Apple CloudKit. We cannot access your iCloud data.

Your data is never uploaded to any of our servers. We do not maintain any backend servers for storing or processing user data.

3. Data Usage

Since we do not collect your personal data, there is no data usage beyond your control. All information you enter and assessment results generated within the App are used solely to provide visa assessment functionality on your device and are never transmitted to us or any third party in any form.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your data with any third parties.

  • We do not sell, rent, or share any information with advertisers, data analytics companies, or other commercial entities.
  • We do not provide data to government agencies or law enforcement (as we do not hold your data).
  • Content you share via the system share sheet (e.g., long-image sharing) is under your full control.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following Apple-provided system services:

  • iCloud (CloudKit): Used for multi-device data synchronization for Pro users. Data is stored in your personal iCloud account and governed by the Apple Privacy Policy.
  • WeatherKit: Used for weather queries within the China Travel Guide feature. No user data is transmitted.
  • In-App Purchases: Uses the Apple StoreKit framework to process subscriptions and purchases. All payment information is handled by Apple; the App does not access your payment information.

Apart from the Apple system services listed above, the App does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising platforms.

6. Data Security

While we do not collect your data, we still value the security of data stored on your device:

  • Local device data is protected by iOS system-level security mechanisms (including data encryption and app sandboxing).
  • iCloud data is protected by Apple ID authentication and encrypted transmission.
  • We recommend using a device passcode, Touch ID, or Face ID to protect your iPhone for enhanced data security.

7. Your Rights

Since all data is stored on your device and personal iCloud account, you always retain full control over your data:

  • Access: You can view your assessment records and materials within the App at any time.
  • Deletion: You can delete assessment records and materials within the App, or remove app data via iPhone Settings → General → iPhone Storage.
  • Stop Syncing: You can disable iCloud Sync in the Me page.
  • Complete Removal: Uninstalling the App will delete all local data; deleting associated data in iCloud will permanently remove it.

8. Children's Privacy

This App is suitable for users of all ages (App Store rating 4+). We do not collect any personal information from any user, including children. If you are a parent or guardian and have any questions about the App's data practices, please contact us using the information below.

9. Disclaimer

Visa assessment results provided by this App are based on publicly available visa policy data and statistical models. They are for reference only and do not constitute visa application advice. Actual visa outcomes are independently determined by respective embassies and consulates based on their internal criteria and are unrelated to the App's assessment results.

10. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When significant changes are made, we will notify you appropriately within the App. We recommend reviewing this Privacy Policy periodically for the latest version.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions, suggestions, or complaints regarding this Privacy Policy or the App's data practices:

  • Email: support@jwapp.top

We will respond to your inquiry as soon as possible.
