Work Tracker Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2, 2026

Thank you for using "Work Tracker." We value your privacy, and this policy will help you understand how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

"Work Tracker" is a locally-first salary tracking tool. We do not actively collect any personally identifiable information. The only data you provide includes:

  • Salary configurations you enter (e.g., monthly salary, hourly rate, work hours)
  • Work sessions and slack sessions you record
  • App preference settings you choose (e.g., currency, language, haptic feedback)

This data is stored only on your device locally, unless you enable iCloud sync.

2. iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your work data will be synchronized across your devices via Apple's CloudKit service. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is protected by Apple's privacy policy. We do not access or store your iCloud data.

3. Data Security

All your data is stored locally on your device or synchronized via Apple's encrypted services. We do not transmit data to any third parties, nor do we use it for advertising or analytics.

4. Third-Party Services

This app does not include third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You can at any time:

  • Modify or delete your data within the app
  • Disable iCloud sync through system settings
  • Uninstall the app to delete all local data
